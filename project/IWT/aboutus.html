About Us

With incredible views and excellent service, Hotel Arigana is a luxurious hotel in a great location. no matter whether guests are visiting us for business or pleasure, we take pride in giving them a memorable and pleasant experience.

Each of the beautifully designed rooms and suites at our hotel was created with the comfort of our visitors in mind. We provide a variety of accommodations that satisfy the requirements of any tourist, from comfortable single rooms to roomy suites. Our hotel facilities also include a cutting-edge fitness facility, a soothing spa, and a selection of restaurants to suit every taste.

By providing great service and paying close attention to every little thing, Hotel Arigana aims to go above and beyond its customers' expectations. We have a helpful and friendly staff on hand around-the-clock to make sure every guest has a wonderful time while staying with us.

Hotel Arigana is the ideal place to stay whether you're in town for a weekend break, a business trip, or a special celebration. We're excited to meet you and make sure you have an outstanding hotel experience.
