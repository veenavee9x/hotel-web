FAQ

How do I make a hotel reservation?

Making a reservation is easy. Simply visit our website or mobile app, enter your destination, check-in and check-out dates, and the number of guests. Our system will display a list of available hotels. Choose the one that suits your preferences and follow the prompts to complete the reservation.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation. Visit our website or contact our customer support team with your reservation details, and they will assist you with the necessary changes or cancellations. Please note that cancellation policies may vary depending on the hotel and the rate you selected.

Are the hotel rates displayed per person or per room?

The rates displayed on our website are typically per room, unless otherwise specified. Make sure to check the room details and rate conditions for clarification. Additional charges or taxes may apply, depending on the hotel's policies.

What payment methods are accepted for reservations?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and online payment platforms. During the reservation process, you will be prompted to enter your payment details securely. We ensure the confidentiality and security of your payment information.

Can I request special accommodations, such as a non-smoking room or extra beds?

Absolutely! Our reservation system provides an option to request special accommodations during the booking process. You can specify preferences like non-smoking rooms, extra beds, or any other requirements you may have. We will do our best to fulfill your requests, but please note that these are subject to availability.

How will I receive confirmation of my reservation?

Once you complete your reservation, you will receive a confirmation email with all the details of your booking. It will include your reservation number, hotel information, dates of stay, and any other relevant information. You can also access your reservation details through your account on our website or mobile app.

What should I do if I encounter any issues during the reservation process or have further questions?

If you encounter any issues or have additional questions, our customer support team is available to assist you. You can reach out to them via phone, email, or live chat, and they will be happy to help you with any concerns or inquiries you may have.
